About Us

Welcome to TodoCorner, a personalized todo application developed by KriparajP. TodoCorner is designed to make managing your tasks simple and efficient, with a range of features to enhance your productivity.

Key Features:

  • Personalized Todo Lists: Create and manage your todo lists effortlessly. Each task can be prioritized to ensure you stay on top of what matters most.
  • Cross-Device Accessibility: By logging in, you can access your todos on any device, with all data securely stored in our MongoDB database.
  • Email Reminders: Never miss a deadline with our email reminder feature, which alerts you on the day of your task.
  • Guest Access: Use TodoCorner without logging in. Your todos will be saved on your device, allowing you to experience the appâ€™s core features without creating an account.
  • Inspiration from Typing Monkey: Our clean and user-friendly design is inspired by the cool design of the Typing Monkey site, ensuring a pleasant user experience.
